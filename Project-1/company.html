Nintendo

Nintendo is a Japanese company that started back in 1889 in Kyoto, Japan, making playing cards. During these many years, it grew into a worldwide video game giant, creating consoles and games which have entertained millions around the world.

Important people

Gunpei Yokoi was one of Nintendos most important designers. He created the Game & Watch series and later designed the Game Boy, which became one of the companys most famous products. Yokois approach focused on simple but fun gameplay, making video games accessible and enjoyable for both kids and adults.
