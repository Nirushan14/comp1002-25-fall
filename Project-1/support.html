Support

If you have any issues with your Game Boy, there are many different ways to obtain assistance. You can read through the instruction manual for the basics and find solutions to common problems, such as a blank screen or frozen game, on the Nintendo website. Additionally, make sure that you have spare batteries and that you regularly clean your game cartridges to prevent any problems while playing games.

Trouble shooting

Things like a black screen or a not working game are often resolvable by checking the batteries or trying the cartridge again.

Maintenance

Always keep your Game Boy in a protected area, regularly clean your cartridges, and do not drop your Game Boy.

Online Help & Resources

You can get help on the Nintendo support website. You could also search for online communities with advice for fixing the issue.
